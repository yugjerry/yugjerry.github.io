Yu Gui 桂昱

I am currently a PhD student in the Department of Statistics at the University of Chicago.

My research interest lies in theory and methods in the field of distribution-free statistical learning. I am also interested in the problems with the presence of distribution shift. Besides, I am grateful to have opportunities to work on multiple testing and spectral methods in high-dimensional time series.

Prior to PhD, I graduated from School of the Gifted Young at University of Science and Technology of China and did the research intership under the supervision of Professor Jun S Liu at Harvard.
