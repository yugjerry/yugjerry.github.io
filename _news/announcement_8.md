New preprint [adaptive discovery of effect modification in matched observational studies](https://arxiv.org/abs/2605.09741): a finite-sample valid procedure to identify covariate-interpretable subgroups with exact **subgroup-level FDR** control, robust to unmeasured confounding via sensitivity models and powered by *multiple matched controls*.
